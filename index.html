I am a third year PhD student in bioarchaeology, my research primarily invovles 3D geometric morphometric analysis of the human mandible to determine how the shape has changed over time among British archaeological assemblages. Other areas of research include, paelopathology, epidemiology, ethics and ownership of 3D data and skeletal responces to mechanicala and environmental stimuli
